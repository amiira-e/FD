Monitor customers' behaviour

This option allows you to monitor customers' behavior. You can gain an initial overview of each customer's past transaction history
to obtain insights into the frequency of both fraudulent and non-fraudulent cases associated with each customer.



Fraud detection

This functionality enables the detection of fraudulent transactions. By providing the necessary input features, advanced AI models such as LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) and
Hybrid CNN-LSTM (Convolutional Neural Network - Long Short-Term Memory) are employed to accurately identify instances of fraud.



Anomaly detection

This option allows you to detect anomalies in transactions.
